Memer

This project is my first attempt at making a substantial mobile application. This idea is an interesting twist on regular mobile dating apps; instead of another platform for judging others of the prefered gender, Memer allows individuals to express their opinions of other individuals' memes. It is a free software that allows anyone to upload memes and see what others think.

Currently, this is all my application features. I have many ideas in mind for improving the application and will continue to develop it, but as a busy college student, I can only put so much time into developing my app.

Bestris

Bestris is my personal take on the classic game of Tetris. I began developing Bestris because I dislike how the only available Tetris games either do not have the standard of gameplay that I seek or require countless hours of gameplay to unlock features and are still swarmed with ads. The goal of Bestris is to be an accessible and free way to play Tetris. While this project is still a work in progress, it features all of the basic functionality of Tetris and can be enjoyed by casual Tetris players.
